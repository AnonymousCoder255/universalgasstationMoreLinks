Ode to Luxembourg

Note: This is a work in progress 

								Luxembourg,
								How mighty and majestic you are.
								Luxembourg,
								How your forests and cities are great
								in both number and size.

								O' Luxembourg, how ever will they see
								how good you can be.
								O' Luxembourg, borne of a treaty.
								
								How could they have done this to you?
								They sliced you up in two,
								Now to get to anoth'r, you'd need a queue.
								Just because they're fight'n anoth'r war,
								Doesn't mean they get to take from you more.
